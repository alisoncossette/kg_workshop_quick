Quick Start Guide:
1. Power button located on top-right corner
2. Initial setup takes 5-10 minutes
3. Connect to WiFi to complete setup

Specifications:
- Processor: Intel i7 12th Gen

Troubleshooting:
If laptop doesn't start:
1. Check power connection
2. Hold power button for 10 seconds
3. Contact support if issue persists
